ABOUT US

OUR RATIONAL

Did you know that Miami is the second least-connected large city in the U.S.? It is our goal to help fix this issue by teaching Miami residents about all the wonderful resources in our city. In this way, we encourage Miami residents to interact with each other and the organizations that make Miami special, strengthening our connections.

Our Plan

  • Step 1: Compile some starting resources for people to discover.
  • Step 2: Miami residents share new resources through our website.
  • Step 3: Miami residents become more connected over the their shared experiences.
  • Step 4: People obtain the support they need to better themselves and Miami.

We hope to make everyone who has struggled in Miami feel included.
